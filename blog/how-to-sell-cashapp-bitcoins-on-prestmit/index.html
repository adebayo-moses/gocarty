A lot of customers have asked about how they can easily withdraw their bitcoins from Cashapp and convert to naira instantly.

In this guide, I will be putting you through how you can trade cashapp bitcoins for naira on Prestmit.

HOW TO SEND BITCOINS FROM CASHAPP TO PRESTMIT WALLET

1. Click on the banking tab on your Cashapp home screen

2. Select Bitcoin

3. Click on Withdraw Bitcoin

4. Enter your Prestmit Bitcoin Address: For those who are yet to get a Prestmit address, it’s easy. Simply login to your account and click on “Bitcoin Wallet“, then generate your permanent bitcoin address and QR code. Any bitcoins sent to this address will reflect on your account.

5. After you have pasted your Prestmit bitcoin address, confirm your phone pin or wallet ID.

6. Sent!

The bitcoin sent will immediately reflect in your Prestmit wallet as “Pending Balance”. Wait a few minutes for it to be confirmed on the bitcoin network.

Once received, it will be converted to naira directly and added to your Prestmit wallet.

Withdrawals are instant as always.
